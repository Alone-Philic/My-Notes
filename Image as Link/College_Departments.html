CHMKM Govt. Arts and Science College

C.H.M.K.M Govt. Arts and Science College Tanur was established in 2013 as a part of establishing new Arts and Science Colleges in Assembly constituencies where there is no Govt. or Aided college. The College now affiliated to the University of Calicut.

DEPARTMENTS :

  • BCA
  • BBA
  • BSc. ELECTRONICS
  • MA MALAYALAM
  • BA ENGLISH
  • BCOM
